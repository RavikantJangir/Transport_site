Welcome To Safe Express Logistics

Safe Express Logistics (SEL) is a professionally managed transportation and logistics company headquartered in Wagholi, Pune.

Established in 2024 by Mr. Somveer Choudhary, the company is committed to providing reliable, cost-effective and time-bound logistics solutions across India.
